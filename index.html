Projects

Otto Group (E-Commerce) Product Classification Challenge

Given a training data set of 62,000 products with 93 feature variables, and 9 target classes, I was challenged to predict the classes of a test set with 145,000 products.

I developed three models: random forest, light gradient boosting, and a multi-layer perceptron classifier.

Overall, the LightGBM model performed the best with a logarithmic loss score of 0.47. This model performed in the top third of Kaggle entries.

Cyclone Classification with Deep Convolutional Neural Networks

Developed two binary image classifiers with convolutional neural networks in TensorFlow, and PyTorch.

Pre-processed the images into tensor batches, scaled the images, split into test-train sets and used resampling for validation sets.

Overall, achieved 82% accuracy with 200 image dataset of hurricanes and non-hurricanes (clouds, smoke).
